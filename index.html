⚔ INSTRUCTIONS ⚔

This solver is designed to find the shortest and most optimal sequence of plate shifts to pick locks in Gothic 1 Remake. This solver models the behavior of lockpicking mechanics from the game and supports configuring dependency rules (when moving one plate also shifts others).

Quick Start Instructions:

  1. Toggle "On Top" (PiP mode) in the header to keep the solver window visible over your game.
  2. Use the arrow buttons to set the current visual offsets of the lock plates (as they appear in the game).
  3. Set up custom lock rules if plates move together. Click "Rules" in the top right to configure which plates shift and their direction when moving a plate left, and click "Save".
  4. If the lock has fewer than 7 plates, leave the unused plates at "0". They will not be counted in the solution.
  5. Click "Find Solution" to calculate the path.
  6. Follow the step-by-step list of moves, or use playback controls (prev / next) to visually step through the solution.
  7. Click "Reset" to return all plates to zero and clear rules.
